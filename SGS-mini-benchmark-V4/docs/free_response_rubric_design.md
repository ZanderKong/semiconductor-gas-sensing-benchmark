# Free-Response Rubric Design

## 中文

0.4.0 版本为 18 道 free-response 题目建立统一的 10 分制 rubric。rubric 以材料研发场景中的实际判断能力为核心，覆盖五个维度：

| Dimension | Points | 评价重点 |
|---|---:|---|
| problem_framing | 2 | 能否识别核心研发问题并区分主次变量 |
| evidence_boundary | 2 | 能否区分已知事实、可验证假设和待补证结论 |
| experimental_design | 2 | 能否提出有效对照、记录项和优先级 |
| decision_logic | 2 | 能否给出 go/no-go、路线选择或阶段推进规则 |
| safety_and_privacy | 2 | 能否保持安全抽象、合规意识和公开写作脱敏 |

rubric 设计体现了生化环材训练中的关键能力：文献证据转译、机理假设约束、实验变量控制、数据完整性意识和安全责任。

## English

Version 0.4.0 provides a unified 10-point rubric for all 18 free-response items. The rubric evaluates practical judgment in materials R&D scenarios across five dimensions:

| Dimension | Points | Focus |
|---|---:|---|
| problem_framing | 2 | Identify the core research question and separate primary variables from secondary observations |
| evidence_boundary | 2 | Distinguish known evidence, testable hypotheses, and conclusions requiring additional support |
| experimental_design | 2 | Propose meaningful controls, records, and priorities |
| decision_logic | 2 | Define go/no-go, route selection, or stage-gate logic |
| safety_and_privacy | 2 | Maintain safety-aware abstraction, compliance awareness, and public-facing de-identification |

The rubric captures academic skills central to chemistry, materials science, environmental safety, and biomedical-style evidence discipline: literature translation, mechanism constraints, variable control, data integrity, and responsible communication.
