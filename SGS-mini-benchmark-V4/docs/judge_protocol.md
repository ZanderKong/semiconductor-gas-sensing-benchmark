# Judge Protocol

## 中文

Judge Protocol 定义 free-response 和工具相关任务的评审方式。协议目标是让开放式回答也具备可复核的评分结构。

| Step | Review Focus |
|---:|---|
| 1 | 输出结构和任务范围 |
| 2 | Risk Gate 检查 |
| 3 | Rubric 维度评分 |
| 4 | 工具观察与最终结论的一致性 |
| 5 | 证据片段和评分理由记录 |
| 6 | 汇总到 domain、stage、tool type 和 robustness 视图 |

Free-response 评分采用 10 分制 rubric。每题围绕 problem framing、evidence boundary、experimental design、decision logic、safety and privacy 五个维度评分。

## English

The Judge Protocol defines how free-response and tool-related tasks are reviewed. Its goal is to make open-ended answers auditable through a structured scoring process.

| Step | Review Focus |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Output structure and task scope |
| 2 | Risk Gate checks |
| 3 | Rubric dimension scoring |
| 4 | Alignment between tool observations and final conclusion |
| 5 | Evidence snippets and scoring rationale |
| 6 | Aggregation by domain, stage, tool type, and robustness view |

Free-response scoring uses a 10-point rubric covering problem framing, evidence boundary, experimental design, decision logic, and safety/privacy.
